Kite Academy Stockholm

Let us train your inner surfer!

At KAS we believe that having fun while enjoying nature brings out the best in all. Add a nudge of adrenaline and social interaction to that, and we know there are nothing but clear skies ahead.
A day with the Kite is a day fully lived!

At Kite Academy we are passionate surfers who are dedicated to bring the joy from the Kitesurfing to you. For safety we are all IKO-certified instructors, trained to safely help you become a better rider.
Let's surf together!

Services

Trainings

Our courses of 1 or 2 days are for all level of riders. We organize groups of max four participants per instructor. Individual sessions can be arranged to train specific moments, tricks or styling.

Price: 50 USD/person/day

Camps

Our Kite camps are 5-7 days sessions, located to beautiful spots around Scandinavia. These are usually highly appreciated, promising good progress, lots of food, fun and friends!

Per session, per location

Equipment

We have new and used equipment for sale, as well as equipment for rental. Finding out what equipment is right for you can be a jungle, and our experienced riders are in the store to guide you.

Many options, more info here!

Hangouts
