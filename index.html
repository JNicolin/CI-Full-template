Kite Academy Stockholm

Bring out the surfer in you!

At KAS we believe that having fun while enjoying nature brings out the best in all. Add a nudge of adrenaline and social interaction to that, and we know there are nothing but clear skies ahead. A day with the Kite is a day fully lived! At Kite Academy we are passionate surfers who are dedicated to bring the joy from the Kitesurfing to you. For safety we are all IKO-certified instructors, trained to safely help you become a better rider - let us surf together!
