Equipment

Let us guide you to the right combination of gear!

Selecting the right Kite equipment is key to make the most of your riding. Having the right gear is a question of safety as well as of getting the performance that matches your objectives. At Kite academy Stockholm we regularly meet with the larger suppliers, we test and review equipment. No matter your level of experience or ambition, we will guide you to find out exactly what suits your needs.

To Buy or to Try?

We offer the options to buy new or used equipment along with an option to rent your equipment for a period. Every year we have dialogues with suppliers to evaluate and pick the gear that we find suitable for Scandinavian conditions. We do support the re-use of equipment that is well kept and that we find to be in good in enough condition to safely pass on to a next rider.

Rental is a popular choice among new riders or for those just passing by Stockholm for a shorter period. Renting your gear is a fun way of exploring and experiencing what can be right for your needs before making a decision to buy.
