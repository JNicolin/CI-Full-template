Equipment

Let us guide you to the right combination of gear!

Selecting the right Kite equipment is key to make the most of your riding. Having the right gear is a question of safety as well as of getting the performance that matches your objectives. At Kite academy Stockholm we regularly meet with the larger suppliers, we test and review equipment. No matter your level of experience or ambition, we will help you find what you need. We offer the options to buy new or used equipment along with an option to rent your equipment for a period.
